Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free trial?
All Professional plan features for 14 days. No credit card required. Full access to all 5 MCP governance tools with 50 analyses included. Your dedicated onboarding specialist will help you get started.
Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?
Yes. Changes take effect immediately. When upgrading, you'll receive prorated credit for the remainder of your current billing period. No penalties for downgrades.
What deployment options are available?
SaaS (multi-tenant cloud), VPC (dedicated instance on AWS/Azure/GCP), On-Premises (behind your firewall), and Hybrid (including air-gapped support for defence and government environments).
How does CASA certification work?
CASA (Certified AI Safety Assurance) is CSGA's governance framework. Each tier includes a CASA level — from CA-10 (awareness) through CA-40 (defence-grade). Certification ensures every AI analysis meets documented safety and compliance standards.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes. Annual plans save 20%. Multi-year agreements and large deployment discounts are available for Enterprise customers. Contact our sales team for custom pricing tailored to your organisation's needs.

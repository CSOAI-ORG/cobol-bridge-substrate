Incident Reporting

Compliance Timeline

DORA Implementation Roadmap

Key milestones for achieving full DORA compliance across your legacy COBOL infrastructure.

Phase 1 — Assessment (Weeks 1–4)

ICT Risk Discovery & Mapping

Deploy MCP Copybook Parser and CICS Bridge Assessment tools across your mainframe estate. Automated discovery of all COBOL programs, copybook dependencies, CICS transaction flows, and JCL batch schedules. Generate initial ICT risk register.

Phase 2 — Framework (Weeks 5–8)

ICT Risk Management Framework

Implement DORA-compliant risk management policies. Configure automated monitoring for COBOL runtime anomalies, CICS abend detection, and JCL failure patterns. Establish incident classification and escalation procedures.

Phase 3 — Testing (Weeks 9–12)

Resilience Testing Programme

Execute threat-led penetration testing for legacy systems. Validate mainframe failover procedures, CICS region recovery, and batch restart capabilities. Document test results and remediation plans for regulatory reporting.

Phase 4 — Ongoing

Continuous Compliance Monitoring

Automated 4-hour incident detection and reporting. Continuous third-party risk monitoring. Regular resilience testing cycles. Real-time compliance dashboard with regulatory reporting automation.
